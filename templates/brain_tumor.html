Types of Brain Tumor

  • Glioma: Tumors from glial cells, typically with blurred boundaries.
  • Meningioma: From meninges, often near brain surface/skull.
  • Pituitary: In the pituitary gland, at the brain base.
  • No Tumor: No tumor detected; scan seems normal.
