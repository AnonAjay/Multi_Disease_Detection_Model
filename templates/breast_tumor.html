Understanding Breast Cancer

  • Tumor found: This suggests abnormal cells or masses are present. Consult a healthcare professional for confirmation and treatment planning.
  • No tumor found: No abnormal signs detected. However, regular screenings are important for early detection.
What is breast cancer?
Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast tissue grow uncontrollably to form tumors, often detectable on scans or medical images.

Why does it happen so often?
Multiple factors contribute: genetics, hormonal changes, age, lifestyle, and environmental factors. It is especially common in women but can affect men too, making regular checkups and awareness key to prevention and early treatment.
