About gastro diseases:
Gastrointestinal diseases can range from benign inflammation (esophagitis) to chronic conditions (ulcerative colitis), and potentially pre-cancerous findings like polyps.

Why is early detection important?
Many GI conditions can be improved or cured with early intervention. Polyp removal reduces colon cancer risk, and timely treatment of colitis or esophagitis enhances patient quality of life.
